 # Recipe
1. Combine the pecorino and cracked pepper in a bowl
2. Add tonnarelli to boiling salted water
3. Ladle pasta water into the cheese mixture and whisk vigorously until the sauce has reached a creamy consistency
4. Once the tonnarelli has cooked, toss the pasta and sauce together in a separate pan until the pasta is coated with sauce
5. Serve and finish with more cracked pepper and pecorino
